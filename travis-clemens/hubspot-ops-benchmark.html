Section 01
Salary Bands — All Markets
Gross monthly salary · Formal employment · Brazil, Argentina, Chile, Uruguay

The four markets present a broadly unified talent cost band for this profile. Despite different local labour structures, a mid-level HubSpot Operations Specialist with strong English, 2–4 years of hands-on experience, and remote delivery capability lands within a comparable range across the region. The differentiators are availability of talent, currency stability, and local employment cost structure — not gross salary.

Mid-Level · 2–4 years · HubSpot experience · Strong English USD 1,500 – 2,200 / mo
Brazil and Chile trend toward the upper end of this band. Argentina and Uruguay sit in the lower-to-mid range. Profiles with active HubSpot certifications or prior B2B SaaS agency experience command a premium within band.

Nearshoring Saving vs US

Hiring the equivalent profile from LATAM represents a 60–70% cost reduction on gross salary compared to a US-based hire — before factoring in employment taxes, benefits, and overheads on both sides. The gap widens further when total employer cost is calculated.
