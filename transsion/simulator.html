How to read this simulator
All statutory employer charges and benefits are pass-through costs — billed to the client at actual cost with no markup. Wide Brazil charges its service fee separately.

Hover over any line item to see a detailed explanation of that cost. The annual provisions row is expanded by default to show the 13th salary, vacation pay and constitutional bonus broken out individually.
